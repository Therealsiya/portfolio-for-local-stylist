About Me

My name is Nhlanhla Mwelase alos known as "asap_pantsula" I was born and bred in Soweto,Baragwana Hospital but moved to Krugersdrop, 45 mintues drive , west of Johanessburg,Gauteng.My content is basically inspired and influenced by my childhood expriences of growing up in Kagiso, a life, sprawling township adjacent to my hometown of Krugersdrop.In 2017, I fell in love with skateboarding and I joined a group of guys from my hood who goes by names of Kearabetswe "Rabbit" Mathibe and Lawrence "Tash" Seobe introduced me to skate wear and the skateboarding culture.In 2018, I was fascinated by the panstula sub-culture, I started wearing Dickies work wear and Chuck 70s, until I met a group called 25 Panstulas and created based on the panstula sub-culture and ever since then creating content is close to my heart.
